Getting
Here

Upper Assam is more accessible than you think. Here's everything you need to know about reaching our three heritage bungalows.

Arrival Options

How to Reach Us

All three bungalows are located within 30–90 minutes of Dibrugarh. We can arrange all transfers from your point of arrival.

By Air

Dibrugarh Mohanbari Airport (DIB) is the nearest airport, well-connected with daily flights from Delhi, Kolkata, and Guwahati.

From Delhi: ~2.5 hours direct flight
From Kolkata: ~1.5 hours direct flight
From Guwahati: ~1 hour direct flight

We arrange airport transfers from Dibrugarh Airport to all three bungalows.

By Rail

Dibrugarh Town Railway Station is connected to major Indian cities. Overnight trains from Delhi (New Delhi Rajdhani) and Guwahati make rail travel comfortable.

From Delhi: ~32 hours via train
From Guwahati: ~8-10 hours

We arrange railway station pickups from Dibrugarh Town.

By Road

Assam's road network connects Dibrugarh to the rest of the Northeast. Self-drive options are available from Guwahati (~450 km, 10 hours).

We recommend road travel as part of a larger Northeast itinerary — the drive through Kaziranga and the tea districts is spectacular.

Travel Advisory

Indian nationals require a valid ID (Aadhaar, passport, or voter ID) for domestic travel within Assam. International guests should check current visa requirements and Inner Line Permit (ILP) regulations for travel to Assam — particularly for stays extending beyond major cities. Our team will advise and assist with ILP applications.
