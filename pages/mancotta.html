Mancotta Heritage
Tea Bungalow

Where colonial charm meets Assamese warmth

4 Rooms Tea Tours Birding Families Welcome Heritage Certified

Mancotta stands at the edge of a sprawling tea estate, its wide verandas facing rows of tea bushes that stretch to the horizon. Built in the colonial era for tea planters, the bungalow retains its original teak floors, high ceilings, and the unhurried pace of a bygone age. Mornings begin with the sound of plucker songs carried on the wind. Evenings end with tea sourced from the very estate you wake up in.

The bungalow offers four spacious rooms, each named after a different flush season — First Flush, Spring, Second Flush, and Autumn — reflecting the rhythm of the tea that defines this land.
