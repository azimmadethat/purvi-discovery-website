The Purvi Discovery Story

2000

Pioneering heritage
stays in Assam

For over two decades, Purvi Discovery has offered travelers something rare — a chance to step into a living history. Our three colonial-era tea bungalows are not simply places to sleep. They are portals to a time when life moved at the pace of a plucker in the garden, when conversations stretched into golden evenings, and when the simplest pleasures — a hot cup of tea, the call of a gibbon at dawn — were enough.

Beyond the Bungalow

Curated Experiences

Each day unfolds at your pace. A tea tour at dawn, a walk through gibbon country, an evening spent listening to stories from Singpho elders.

Tea Tours

Walk the rows of second-flush Assam tea with the workers who have tended these gardens for generations. End with a tasting that will change how you see your morning cup.

Explore Tea Tours

Country Walks & Birding

Walk through Maguri Beel wetlands and Dihing Patkai forests, home to over 400 species of birds. Or stroll Barekuri village at dawn, where the endangered Hollock gibbon swings overhead.

Explore Walks & Birding

Culture & Community

Visit the Singpho tribe at Ingthong village, learn mekhla chadar weaving, and celebrate Bihu with local communities. Travel that leaves the people you meet better for having met you.
